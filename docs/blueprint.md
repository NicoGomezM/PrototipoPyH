## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#22427B) reflecting trust and reliability in the transportation industry.
- Background color: A light gray (#F0F2F5) to provide a clean and modern backdrop, ensuring readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant orange (#E57200) to highlight calls to action and important information, adding a touch of energy.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font for a clean and modern look suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent services and navigation items.
- Implement a grid-based layout for a structured and easy-to-navigate design.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
